# 🌱 Green Vision AI – Plant Disease Detection & Forecasting System

## Project Overview

Green Vision AI is an AI-powered collaborative platform developed to automate plant disease detection, monitoring, and forecasting in real time. The system integrates deep learning, image processing, and environmental analytics to help farmers identify crop diseases at an early stage and take preventive actions before significant crop damage occurs.

The project aims to bridge the gap between traditional manual crop inspection methods and modern intelligent agricultural systems by providing scalable, data-driven, and predictive solutions.

## Main Idea Behind the Project

Plant diseases are one of the primary reasons for reduced agricultural productivity and economic losses worldwide. Traditional disease diagnosis methods rely heavily on manual inspection and expert knowledge, making them:

- Time-consuming
- Labor-intensive
- Expensive
- Difficult to scale
- Inefficient for real-time monitoring

Green Vision AI addresses these challenges by leveraging Artificial Intelligence to automatically detect diseases from plant images and forecast future outbreaks using environmental and historical data.

## Project Framework

### Step 1: Image Acquisition
Plant images are collected using mobile devices, cameras, or drones.

### Step 2: Image Preprocessing
The captured images undergo:

- Noise removal
- Image enhancement
- Segmentation
- Feature extraction

### Step 3: Disease Detection
CNN models analyze plant leaf images and classify healthy and diseased plants.

### Step 4: Disease Forecasting
RNN and LSTM models utilize historical and environmental data such as:

- Temperature
- Humidity
- Weather patterns
- Previous disease occurrences

to predict future outbreaks.

### Step 5: Recommendation System
The system provides:

- Suitable pesticide recommendations
- Preventive measures
- Crop management suggestions

### Step 6: Monitoring & Alerts
Real-time notifications are sent to farmers regarding:

- Disease detection
- Crop health status
- Forecasted disease outbreaks
